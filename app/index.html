You're leaving The Square?

If you insist on going, check out Steubenville's 20+ murals, or see historic Fort Steuben. Hungry? Grab a sausage sandwich from Capri, or try the hand made pasta at Scaffidi's. Wash it all down with some margaritas at Puerto.
