Bennigan’s

Bennigan’s is a high-energy neighborhood restaurant and tavern that is redefining casual dining. With chef-driven food, innovative drinks and warm, friendly Irish hospitality, this Legendary brand delivers memorable dining experiences to every guest.

Brooklyn Bagel Co.

A New-York style bagel shop offering a variety of plain and flavorful bagels, hot-off-the-grill breakfast sandwiches, and signature pastries. Additionally, Brooklyn Bagel on the Square will feature coffee from La Prima Espresso Italiano, a Pittsburgh favorite, specializing in high quality espressos, lattes, cold brew, cappuccino, and drip coffees. Brooklyn Bagel on the Square is slated to open Autumn 2017.

Rubi’s Pizza and Grill

A new restaurant from the family-owned Corrado’s Pizza in Steubenville. Rubi’s Pizza and Grill features 30 varieties of pizza, Italian stromboli, calzone, chicken wings, charbroiled hamburgers, sub sandwiches, pasta and Italian salads, plus Italian desserts. Rubi’s Pizza and Grill is slated to open Autumn 2017
